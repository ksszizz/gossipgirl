Meet the Upper East Side Elite

Serena van der Woodsen

The IT girl of the Upper East Side — effortlessly glamorous, unpredictable, and magnetic. Serena’s beauty and charm draw everyone in, but her impulsive choices and tangled relationships keep her life anything but perfect. She’s always chasing freedom… and sometimes running from her past.

"Some girls are born to stand out."

Blair Waldorf

Queen B — ambitious, stylish, and razor-sharp. Blair is the definition of Upper East Side royalty, ruling Constance Billard with her signature headbands and scheming smile. Beneath the perfectionism and power plays lies a dreamer who believes in true love, destiny, and a perfect fairytale ending.

"Destiny is for losers."

Chuck Bass

The bad boy billionaire with a dangerous charm. Chuck is indulgent, complicated, and heartbreakingly human beneath his designer suits. He hides pain behind power and pleasure — but when it comes to love, especially Blair, he’s willing to risk everything.

"I'm Chuck Bass."

Nate Archibald

The golden boy with a heart of gold. Nate’s charm and looks make him everyone’s dream, but he’s more than a rich kid — he’s loyal, honest, and constantly searching for purpose beyond privilege. Always caught between love and loyalty, Nate stays true to his moral compass.

"I'm not a bad guy

Dan Humphrey

The Lonely Boy, who dared to enter the Upper East Side world. Smart, introspective, and often judgmental, Dan is both observer and participant — trying to fit into a society he claims to despise. His words cut deep, whether in his writing or his secret identity.

"Every story starts with an outsider."

Jenny Humphrey

Little J who traded innocence for ambition. Jenny started as a dreamer with a sewing machine and ended up fighting for a place among Manhattan’s elite. She’s talented, fearless, and willing to risk everything for the crown — but the higher she climbs, the more she learns that power always comes at a price.

"I'm not a little girl anymore."

Vanessa Abrams

The Brooklyn girl with an artist’s soul and a rebel’s heart. Vanessa sees the Upper East Side for what it really is — beautiful, ruthless, and impossible to ignore. She wants truth in a world built on lies, but even she can’t resist getting pulled into the glittering chaos she once judged.

"I see the world they pretend to live in."

Georgina Sparks

The storm you never see coming. Georgina is unpredictable, wickedly intelligent, and terrifyingly charming. She thrives on chaos and always shows up when things are about to explode. Love her or fear her — she’s the kind of girl who doesn’t need an invitation to the party; she is the party.

"Sweetheart, I am the scandal."

And who am I? That's one secret I'll never tell...You know you love me.
